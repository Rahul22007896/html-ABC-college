Computer Science

Embark on a journey of innovation and excellence with the Department of Computer Science and Engineering, a cornerstone of academic prowess of Saveetha Engineering College since its inception in 2001. What began with an intake of 60 students has evolved into a dynamic hub of learning, now accommodating 240 bright minds by the year 2023-24. Our commitment to growth is evident in our consistent expansion, from 120 students in 2005-2006 to 180 in 2017-18. At our department, we nurture talents through our esteemed B.E. and M.E. programs in Computer Science and Engineering. Guided by a team of 36 dedicated faculty members, comprising seasoned professionals with diverse expertise, we ensure a nurturing environment for academic excellence. Among them, 13 hold doctorates, while others are actively pursuing their Ph.D.s, embodying our commitment to staying at the forefront of research and knowledge.
